About This Application

  • Privacy First: This application runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
  • Local Storage: Your checklists are saved in browser cookies, which are stored only on your device.
  • File Management: You can save checklists to files on your computer and load them back later.
  • Cookie Usage: Cookies are used only to remember your checklists between visits. They expire after one year.
  • Offline Capable: Once loaded, this application works without an internet connection.
